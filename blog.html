The History of Artificial Inteligence

OpenAI, the artificial intelligence research organization founded by Elon Musk, claimed one of many to come world first: its artificial intelligence system trained to play the complex strategy game Dota 2 has bested a world champion e-sports team. The competition was held in the bay area and dubbed the OpenAI Five Finals, ending the organization’s public demonstrations of its Dota-playing technology on a high note.


The competition on the human side included five top Dota 2 pros from team OG, which won the world’s most extreme e-sports prize last year when it took the Number one spot at The International competition, the premiere annual Dota 2 tournament with prizes now reaching $25 million usd. OG faced off in a best of three matches against the OpenAI Five bots, all trained using the same deep reinforcement learning techniques and controlled independently by different layers of the same system.
